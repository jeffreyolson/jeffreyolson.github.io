Adams: I Am Going There

“Adapted to a favorite melody,” this song was published by John Adams in 1852 with the subtitle “The Death of Little Eva” and a dedication “To the readers of Uncle Tom's Cabin”. The title quotes Miss Eva as she points to heaven from her death bed while Uncle Tom comforts her.
