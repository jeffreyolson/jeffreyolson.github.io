Schubert: Wiegenlied (Cradle Song) D 498

Schubert's melody for his 1816 Wiegenlied (Cradle Song) is less well known than Brahms' 1868 Wiegenlied, but Schubert's was reused with Italian lyrics as “Mille cherubini in coro” for a 1935 film.
