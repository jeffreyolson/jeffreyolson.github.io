American: A Tisket, A Tasket

A-tisket a-tasket
A green and yellow basket
I wrote a letter to my friend
And on the way I dropped it.

I dropped it, I dropped it,
And on the way I dropped it.
A little boy he picked it up
And put it in his pocket.
