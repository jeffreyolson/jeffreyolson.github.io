Dacre: Daisy Bell (A Bicycle Built For Two)

This was the first song sung by a computer using voice synthesis back in 1961 at Bell Labs, so Arthur C. Clarke used it in his 1968 movie “2001: A Space Odyssey” when the murderous HAL 9000 computer is being regressed to a harmless, child-like state.

Daisy, Daisy,
Give me your answer, do!
I'm half crazy,
All for the love of you!
It won't be a stylish marriage,
I can't afford a carriage,
But you'll look sweet upon the seat
Of a bicycle built for two!
