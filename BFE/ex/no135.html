Waldteufel: España Waltz

This 1886 piece was inspired by Emmanuel Chabrier's 1883 “España” and both inspired the popular 1956 song “Hot Diggity, Dog Ziggity, Boom, What You Do To Me! (when You're Holding Me Tight)”, which you can hear in the youtube below.
