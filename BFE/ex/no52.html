Terry: Lullay, Lullay

The more familiar “Lully, Lullay” comes from “The Coventry Carol”. This exercise is the more recent melody by Richard Terry.

On yesternight I saw a sight,
A star as bright as day,
And all along I heard a song,
Lullay, Lullay, Lullay.
