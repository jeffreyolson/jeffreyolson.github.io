English: The Golden Vanity

Oh, there was a mighty ship, and she sailed upon the sea,
And the name of the ship was the Golden Vanity.
And they feared she might be taken by the Turkish enemy
That sailed upon the lowland, lowland sea,
That sailed upon the lowland sea.
