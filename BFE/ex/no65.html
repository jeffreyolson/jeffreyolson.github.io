Beethoven: Marmotte, Op.52 No.7

None other than Johann Wolfgang von Goethe wrote a play in which poor beggar children sing while their pet marmots (similar to ground hogs) perform tricks for money. Beethoven wrote this melody to go with Goethe's lyrics.
