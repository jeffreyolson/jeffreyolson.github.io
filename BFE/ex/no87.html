Bishop: Shoo Fly, Don't Bother Me

The Dinah Shore hit “Shoo-Fly Pie and Apple Pandowdy” about Pennsylvania Dutch cooking didn't come along until 1945, well after this 1860 song.

Shoo, fly, don't bother me,
Shoo, fly, don't bother me,
Shoo, fly, don't bother me,
For I belong to some-bod-y.

I feel, I feel,
I feel like a morning star.
I feel, I feel,
I feel like a morning star.
