Mozart: Don Giovanni, K.527, Là ci darem la mano

“In the opera of the same name, Don Giovanni (a.k.a Don Juan) sings this song to/with the woman he is seducing.

(translation)
Give me thy hand, oh fairest,
Whisper a gentle 'Yes',
Come, if for me thou carest,
With joy my life to bless.
