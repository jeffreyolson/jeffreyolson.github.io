American Cowboy: Jack o' Diamonds

O Mollie, O Mollie,
It is for your sake alone
That I leave my poor parents,
My house and my home.

Jack o' diamonds, Jack o' diamonds,
I know you of old,
You've robbed my poor pockets
Of silver and gold.

I bet the Jack against a deuce,
Tried to play but it wasn't no use.
Jack o' diamonds is a hard card to play.
