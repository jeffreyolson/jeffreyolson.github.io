For playing in two positions: I and VII

You know the drill from the DFE: play each exercise in this group in both seventh and first positions, and remember: “first finger E on fifth string” to find seventh position by listening instead of looking.

Fretboard diagrams for positions I and VII

Colored circles show where to finger the notes that positions I and VII have in common, and measure bars show the normal four-finger ranges for both positions. First position has been extended to include the high A. Notice (and remember) that in position VII three notes ( C♯, G♯, E♭ ) require a stretch, either with finger 4 (as shown) or with finger 1 on the next higher string.
