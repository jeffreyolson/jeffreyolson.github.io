J.J.'s Sightreading Projects

Diatonic Fretboard Exercises

A compendium of over 1200 simple exercises arranged for guitar sightreading, compiled from 500+ public domain melodies that were analyzed by computer to provide a graduated sightreading program covering all the natural notes on the fretboard.

Chromatic Fretboard Exercises

A second compendium of over 1100 more challenging exercises that cover the entire fretboard, including sharps and flats. Most exercises are playable in multiple positions, with computer grading of their difficulty in each position.

Monthly Fretboard Exercises

A collection of one-page sightreading handouts prepared for the Boulder Guitar Society. Each month has a seasonal theme with a whimsical fretboard graphic and a couple of pieces related to that theme on the front, along with entertaining links to wikipedia and youtube performances on the back.
